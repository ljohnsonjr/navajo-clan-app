This application honors Navajo tradition through:

  • The four sacred colors: White (East), Turquoise (South), Yellow (West), and Black (North)
  • Traditional red earth tones complementing the sacred colors
  • Corn (Naadą́ą́'), one of the four sacred plants, decorating the page borders
  • Proper Navajo orthography with high tones (á, é, í, ó)
  • Glottal stops (')
  • Barred L (ł)
  • Nasal vowels (ą, ę, į, ǫ)
